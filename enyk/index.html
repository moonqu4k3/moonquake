What is EniYukie?
EniYukie (a.k.a EniRuy, sometimes abbr. ENYK) is the name of my self-ship/OCxCanon ship with Enishi Yukishiro from Rurouni Kenshin. This page is a sort of shrine/info page/directory of all things ENYK related. I plan to keep a gallery of all my commissioned pieces, self-drawn pieces, and writing about them.
Who is Yukie?
Yukie (given name Ruyanpe) Hokuto is my self-insert OC for Rurouni Kenshin. They were born in modern day Erimo and raised in an Ainu village. After her father's death (caused by slavery) in her early teens, Yukie would attempt to get revenge until they were gravely wounded, creating a shooting star shaped scar and leaving them with minimal usage of their right arm. Yukie would spend the next years as a vagrant, desperately trying to survive in the changing landscape of mainland Japan.
Who is Enishi?
Enishi Yukishiro is the antagonist of the Jinchu Arc of Rurouni Kenshin. He is the younger brother of Tomoe Yukishiro, Kenshin's wife during the Bakumatsu, who would be unfortunately slain by Kenshin during a battle for her life. Enishi witnessed his sister's murder and would be driven mad with rage, spending the next decade and a half planning his "Jinchu" (lit. "human punishment"; derived from "Tenchu" ["heavenly punishment"], a word often seen on assassination letters during the Bakumatsu) against Kenshin. He would eventually rise to the top of the Shanghai Mafia, giving him access to everything he could possibly need to turn Kenshin's life into a living hell.
How They Met
Yukie and Enishi met after Yukie snuck aboard a ship that they believed was heading to Shanghai, as they were more than eager to leave Japan altogether by this point in their life. However, the ship was actually going to Enishi's island hideout to fund his Jinchu plot. After being caught, Yukie is dragged offboard, first meeting the second-in-command of the Shanghai Mafia, Wu Heixing. Letting their audacity get the best of them, Yukie taunts Heixing until he shoots off a part of their right ear. Enishi, carrying trauma of his sister being killed, orders Heixing to stand down.

Yukie and Enishi barter for a while, before Yukie proposes an absurd deal: allow them to remain on his island and they will work as a live-in maid. Partially spurred on by the sickening sight of blood dripping down Yukie's neck, Enishi surprisingly agrees to their deal, under one condition: they will remain out of each other's business. Well... maybe this webpage will help you figure out how that held up.
Relationship Details
Yukie and Enishi suffer from oblivious idiot syndrome. Both believe that there's no way the other has any feelings towards the other, despite the piercing blushes that both of their faces display when they're near each other.

Misc. Tidbits
Yukie is a foil of Tomoe, Enishi's deceased sister. Yukie is nearly everything Tomoe was not, which forces Enishi to grapple with his complex surrounding Tomoe and eventually accepting Yukie as they are, rather than projecting the image of his sister onto them.

Yukie's Names
Yukie's Japanese name, Hokuto Yukie, is written as 北斗 由紀恵. Hokuto is from Hokuto Shichisei, the Japanese word for the Big Dipper, the constellation that is often used to locate the North Star, Polaris. Yukie is written with the kanji for, "reason", "era", and "blessings". Their given Ainu name, Ruyanpe (ルヤンペ when written in katakana), means rain, and comes from their tendency to play in the rain often, and where the rainiest months of the year are often around their birthday.
Animal Motifs
Enishi is canonically associated with a tiger, and Yukie's motif is that of an Eastern buzzard. Enishi's tiger motif is a foil to Kenshin's dragon motif. Yukie's is a buzzard because of their generalist diet, which is reminiscient of Yukie's survival tendencies during their life as a vagrant.
